Module 2: Replicability in Bioinformatics

This module introduces the foundational concepts of reproducibility in bioinformatics research.

← Back to all modules

Learning Objectives

  • Define reproducibility in the context of bioinformatics research
  • Identify characteristics of reproducible computational workflows
  • Recognize common barriers to reproducibility
  • Apply reproducibility best practices to a research project

Reflection: The Scientific Method and Reproducibility

Reflect on the following question(s) before moving on:

  1. Consider a time when instructions you were given to complete a task were unclear. How did that impact your ability to complete the task?
  2. How could a person ensure that others would be able to reproduce or replicate their computational analyses?
  3. What small adjustments might make a computational workflow clearer and easier to reproduce?
