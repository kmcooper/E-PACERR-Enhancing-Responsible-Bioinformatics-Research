Module 12: Professionalism in Bioinformatics

This module introduces professional norms and practices that support responsible, collaborative, and sustainable bioinformatics research.

← Back to all modules

Learning Objectives

  • Identify core elements of professionalism in bioinformatics (communication, reliability, accountability, respect)
  • Describe best practices for collaboration, authorship, and credit in computational research
  • Recognize common sources of conflict (deadlines, scope, expectations) and strategies to address them
  • Apply responsible practices for managing projects, data, code, and documentation over time
