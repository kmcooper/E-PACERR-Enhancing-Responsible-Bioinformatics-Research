Statistics Knowledge Check

Answer these questions to check your basic statistical knowledge.

1. Consider a dataset of numbers. What is the mean of a dataset?


The mean is the arithmetic average (sum of values divided by number of values).
2. What is the median?


The median is the middle value when numbers are sorted.
3. What is the mode?


The mode is the value that appears most frequently.
4. What do descriptive statistics do?


Descriptive statistics summarize data using measures like mean, median, and graphs.
5. What do inferential statistics do?


Inferential statistics use sample data to make conclusions about larger populations.

This check helps determine if you are ready for Module 3 statistical content.
