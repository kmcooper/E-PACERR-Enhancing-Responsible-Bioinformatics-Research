Module 9: Standards for Excellence in Bioinformatics Education & Training

This module introduces principles and standards that support high-quality bioinformatics education and training, including clear learning goals, inclusive design, and assessment.

← Back to all modules

Learning Objectives

  • Identify key components of effective bioinformatics instruction (objectives, practice, feedback)
  • Describe inclusive and accessible teaching practices relevant to computational learning
  • Explain how assessment and rubrics support transparency and fairness
  • Recognize ways to evaluate and improve training materials over time
