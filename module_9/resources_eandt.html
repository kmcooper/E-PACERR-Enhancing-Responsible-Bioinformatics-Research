Freely Available Online Bioinformatics Training Resources

← Back to Module

Please review the list below of major publicly available online training resources for bioinformatics and computational biology. While there is currently no formal bioinformatics certification or licensure that can ensure a trainee has a specific set of bioinformatics skills, the list below can be used to survey the landscape of possible skills that are possible in the field.

Resource Anticpated Audience Organization / Host Approximate Time Commitment Website
EMBL-EBI Training On Demand All levels European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI) Unlimited/Multiple hours of content ebi.ac.uk/training/online
ELIXIR TeSS Training Materials All levels ELIXIR Europe Unlimited/Multiple hours of content https://tess.elixir-europe.org/materials
Galaxy for Scientists Training All levels Galaxy Project Unlimited/Multiple hours of content training.galaxyproject.org
NCBI Learn All levels National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI) Multiple tutorials and webinar announcement feed ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/home/learn
Rosalind All levels interested in bioinformatics coding tasks and problems Rosalind Project Over 100 coding projects/challenges rosalind.info
Coursera Bioinformatics Specialization (Audit) Undergraduate through graduate University of California San Diego 7 course series coursera.org
Coursera Genomic Data Science (Audit) Undergraduate through graduate Johns Hopkins University 6 course series coursera.org
Data Carpentry: Genomics Beginner The Carpentries Designed as a multi-day workshop datacarpentry.org
Software Carpentry Beginner programmers The Carpentries Designed as a multi-day workshop software-carpentry.org
Bioconductor Course Materials All levels Bioconductor 20–40 hrs bioconductor.org
