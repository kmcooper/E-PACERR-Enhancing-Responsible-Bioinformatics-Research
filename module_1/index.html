Module 1: Reproducibility in Bioinformatics

This module introduces the foundational concepts of reproducibility in bioinformatics research, including why reproducible practices matter and how they support scientific rigor.

← Back to all modules

Learning Objectives

  • Define reproducibility in the context of bioinformatics research
  • Identify characteristics of reproducible computational workflows
  • Recognize common barriers to reproducibility
  • Apply basic reproducibility best practices to a research project
